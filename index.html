FAQs

Frequently asked questions about Eastel

1. Who is Eastel and which network does Eastel operate on?
Eastel is managed by Anchor Communications Sdn. Bhd., a fully regulated and certified Network Service Provider and Application Service Provider licensed by SKMM Malaysia. Eastel operates as a Mobile Virtual Network Operator (MVNO) using U Mobile's network infrastructure.
2. What services are provided under Eastel?
As a thick MVNO, Eastel provides prepaid, postpaid, family plans, IoT and co-branding solutions, including eSIM, for voice, SMS, data, reloads, mobile number portability, and value-added services, supported by an incentive-based referral programme.
3. How can customers check network coverage?
Network coverage can be checked via U Mobile's official coverage map and the Eastel App advisory: u.com.my/coverage-map
4. How do I join Eastel?
You can join Eastel by registering a Starter Pack SIM card for RM10.
5. How can I contact Eastel?
Eastel Hotline: 011-70337777 (8:00am – 10:00pm daily)
WhatsApp Support: 011-61601220
Email: support@eastel.com.my
Operation Office: KL Eco City Mall (10:00am – 10:00pm daily)
6. Where can I purchase an Eastel SIM card?
Eastel SIM cards can be purchased via walk-in at the Eastel Operation Office, Hotline (011-70337777), WhatsApp (011-61601220), Email, or the Eastel Mobile Application.
7. What identification documents are required for registration?
Malaysians / Permanent Residents: MyKad, MyPR, MyTentera, or MyKAS. Foreign nationals: Passport.
8. What is the minimum age requirement for registration?
Below 12 years old: not allowed. Ages 12–17: As a mobile user only. Age 18 and above: May register and be an EASTELPRENEUR.
9. How many Eastel SIM cards can be registered under one ID?
Malaysians / Permanent Residents: Up to 5 active Eastel SIM cards under one IC. Foreign nationals: Up to 5 prepaid SIMs across all telco providers. Companies: No limit, subject to company verification.
10. Does the Eastel SIM card support IDD and international roaming?
Yes.
