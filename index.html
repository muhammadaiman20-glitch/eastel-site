Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speeds do you offer?

▼

For basic browsing and email, 25-50 Mbps is sufficient. For 4K streaming and online gaming, we recommend 100-200 Mbps. For large households with multiple devices, 500 Mbps to 1 Gbps ensures smooth performance for everyone.

How long does installation take? ▼

Standard installations typically take 2-4 hours. Complex setups or custom configurations may require additional time. Our technicians will provide an accurate estimate during the site survey.

What is your contract policy? ▼

We offer both contract and no-contract options. Contract plans (12-24 months) often include promotional pricing and free equipment. No-contract plans provide flexibility with month-to-month billing but may have higher monthly rates.

Do you offer technical support? ▼

Yes! We provide 24/7 technical support via phone, email, and live chat. Our support team is based in Malaysia and understands local infrastructure challenges to provide faster resolutions.
